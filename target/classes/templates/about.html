The purpose of blogs

Filter information: It is the era of internet information. There was too much, too complicated, and messy information that are overflooding on the internet. It may cause many learners cannot distinguish the important and useful information. Thus, browsing other's blog can help you to improve the gain of efficiency of knowledge.

Provide a valuable context for learning: Different to the teaching assistant website that only provides links to some reference materials, while the blog provides more evaluations and broader background information.

Improve literacy: During the process of information release, Blogger needs the most appropriate way to filter and explain information to makes his thoughts are acceptable and understood by more peoples.
